A Letter from the Creator

Hello! I'm so glad you are here! Welcome to my little cozy corner of the world. As a natural creator, collector, observer, and teacher, beautiful and inspiring artistry mesmerizes and magnetizes me. Then, a smattering of ideas flow. I have a terrible time trying to focus on one thing long enough to bring it to fruition. Hopefully, the adage that what enters the mind and soul affects what it creates and sends back out to the world is true. This world, all of the Lord's creations, His Plan, and His children, are awe inspiring. I truly hope and pray, in all humility, that I create things which the Lord magnifies, inspires, and uses to His glory. I hope that my works bless and uplift the lives and hearts of all whose paths intersect with mine. May the Lord always bless and keep you. Most sincerely,

Lynnora A.k.a. Joy Lighthouse

Sign-up for my Newsletter & Follow Me!

Following a writer on social media offers a unique and direct way to engage with their creative process and stay updated on their latest work. It provides a behind-the-scenes glimpse into their inspirations, writing journey, and upcoming projects, making fans feel more connected and invested in their success. Writers often share personal insights, exclusive content, and sneak peeks into their works-in-progress, creating a sense of intimacy and excitement. Plus, social media platforms allow readers to interact with writers, ask questions, and participate in discussions, fostering a community of like-minded individuals. Whether it's discovering new writing, gaining valuable tips, or simply supporting a favorite author, following a writer's social media is a great way to stay connected and be part of their creative world.

Subscribing to a writer’s newsletter is a great way to stay intimately connected with their creative world. Newsletters often offer exclusive content that you won’t find anywhere else—whether it's sneak peeks at upcoming projects, early access to new releases, or personal updates about the writer’s life and writing journey. It’s also a direct, more personal way to receive curated thoughts, book recommendations, or even writing tips, delivered straight to your inbox. Unlike social media, newsletters allow for a deeper, more focused connection without the distractions of endless scrolling, creating a dedicated space for meaningful content. By subscribing, you’re not only staying informed, but you’re also supporting the writer’s work in a way that helps them grow and continue creating.

Coming Soon - current works!

The "Soon to Release & Released Works" page on the writer's website will be a central spot for fans to track upcoming and recently launched books. For upcoming releases, the page will feature placeholders with details like anticipated release dates, book covers, and brief synopsis, giving readers a glimpse of what’s to come. These placeholders will be updated as the launch draws near, replacing the "coming soon" content with actual pre-order links, sample chapters, and behind-the-scenes insights as the release date approaches. Once a book is officially released, the page will transition to showcase purchase options, reviews, and media coverage. This evolving space will keep readers informed, creating anticipation and excitement for new works while ensuring they have all the info they need as soon as the book is available.

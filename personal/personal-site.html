A Letter from the Creator

Hello! I'm so glad you are here! Welcome to my little cozy corner of the world. As a natural creator, collector, observer, and teacher, beautiful and inspiring artistry mesmerizes and magnetizes me. Then, a smattering of ideas flow. I have a terrible time trying to focus on one thing long enough to bring it to fruition. Hopefully, the adage that what enters the mind and soul affects what it creates and sends back out to the world is true. This world, all of the Lord's creations, His Plan, and His children, are awe inspiring. I truly hope and pray, in all humility, that I create things which the Lord magnifies, inspires, and uses to His glory. I hope that my works bless and uplift the lives and hearts of all whose paths intersect with mine. May the Lord always bless and keep you. Most sincerely,

Lynnora A.k.a. Joy Lighthouse
